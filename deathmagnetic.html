DEATH MAGNETIC

Death Magnetic is Metallica's tenth studio album, released in September 2008 on Warner Bros. Records.
It is the first album with current Metallica bassist Robert Trujillo and the first to be produced by Rick Rubin.

Songs in the album:That Was Just Your Life, The End of the Line, Broken,Beat&Scarred, The Day That Never Comes All Nightmare Long, Cyanide, The Unforgiven III, The Judass Kiss, Suicide&Redemption, My Apocalypse
