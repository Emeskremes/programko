KILL ´EM ALL

Kill 'Em All is Metallica's first studio album.
To date, it has sold over 4 and a half million copies in the US, and worldwide sales have exceeded the 9 million mark.

Songs in the album: Hit the Lights, Seek & Destroy, The Four Horsmen, Motorbreath, Jump in the Fire, Whiplash, (Anesthesia) - Pulling Teeth, Phantom Lord, No Remorse, Metal Militia
