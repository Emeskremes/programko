72 SEASONS

72 Seasons is Metallica's eleventh studio album.
On 14 April 2023, the album was released as the second album on their own label, Blackened Recordings.
72 Seasons was produced by Greg Fidelman.

Songs in the album: 72 Seasons, Shadows Follow, Screaming Suicide, Sleepwalk My Life Away, You Must Burn!, Lux Æterna, Crown Of Barbed Wire, Chasing Light, If Darkness Had a Son,
Too Far Gone?, Room Of Mirrors, Inamorata
