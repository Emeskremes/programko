THE BLACK

The Black is the fifth studio album by American thrash metal band Metallica.
To date, it has sold over 17 and a half million copies in the USA and worldwide sales have exceeded 35 million.

Songs in the album: Enter Sandman, Sad But True, Holier Than You, The Unforgiven, Wherever I May Roam, Don´t Thread on Me, Through the Never, Nothing Else Matters, Of Wolf and Man,
The God That Failed, My Friend of Misery, The Struggle Within
