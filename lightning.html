RIDE THE LIGHTNING

Ride the Lightning is Metallica's second studio album, released on July 27, 1984.
To date, it has sold over 7 and a half million units in the US, and worldwide sales have surpassed the 15 million mark.

Songs in the album: Fight Fire With Fire, Ride The Lightning, For Whom The Bell Tolls, Escape, Fade To Black, Traped Under Ice, Creeping Death, The Call Of Ktulu
