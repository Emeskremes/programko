ST. ANGER

St. Anger is Metallica's eighth album.
To date, it has sold over 2 million units in the US and worldwide sales have exceeded the 6 million mark.

Songs in the album: Frantic, St.Anger, Some Kind Of Monster, Dirty Window, Invisible Kid, My World, Shoot Me Again, Sweet amber, The Unnamed Feeling, Purify, All Within My Hands
