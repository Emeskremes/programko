Metallica is an American heavy metal band formed in 1981 in Los Angeles.
The founding members met through an advertisement in a Los Angeles newspaper placed by drummer Lars Ulrich.
The core members of the band are drummer Lars Ulrich, vocalist and backing guitarist James Hetfield, and solo guitarist Kirk Hammett.
