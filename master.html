MASTER OF PUPPETS

Master of Puppets is the third album by Metallica, released on March 3, 1986.
The album reached the 29th position in the Top 200 of the "Billboard" magazine.
To date, it has sold 8 million copies in America alone.

Songs in the album : Master of Puppets, Battery, The Thing That Should Not Be, Welcome Home (Sanitarium), Disposable Heroes, Lepper Messiah, Orion, Damage, Inc.
