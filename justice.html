...AND JUSTICE FOR ALL

...And Justice for All is Metallica's fourth studio album, released on August 25, 1988.
It is the first album to feature new bassist Jason Newsted, as well as the first album without original bassist Cliff Burton, due to his death in September 1986.

Songs in the album: Blackened, ...And Justice for All, Eye of the Beholder, One, The Shortest Straw, Harvester of Sorrow, The Frayed Ends of Sanity, To Live Is to Die
